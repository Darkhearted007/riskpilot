Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 1, 2025
Operated by RiskPilot | Lagos, Nigeria | support@riskpilot.app

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how RiskPilot ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our Service at riskpilot-coral.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address and password (for account creation and authentication)
  • Trade data (entry price, stop loss, take profit, lot size, direction, setup type, notes)
  • Account balance and risk parameters entered into the calculator
  • Profile information (name, subscription status)
  • Payment information — processed by Paystack. We do not store card details.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, session duration, click patterns)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, screen resolution)
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Performance data via Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights
  • Advertising data via Meta Pixel (for Facebook/Instagram ad optimization)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, receipts)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Optimize advertising campaigns (Meta Pixel)
  • Comply with legal obligations

✓ We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. We do NOT use your trade data for any purpose other than providing the Service to you.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract performance: processing necessary to provide the Service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: fraud prevention, security, improving the Service
  • Consent: marketing communications and analytics (you may withdraw at any time)
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable laws

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely with the following protections:

  • Row Level Security (RLS): your data is isolated — only you can access it
  • Encrypted connections: all data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Password hashing: passwords are never stored in plain text
  • PCI DSS compliance: payment processing via Paystack meets card industry standards
  • Access controls: strict internal access controls on production systems

We implement industry-standard security measures. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete your account:

  • Personal profile data is deleted within 30 days
  • Trade journal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Payment records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely

7. Cookies and Tracking

RiskPilot uses the following types of cookies and tracking:

  • Essential cookies: required for authentication and session management
  • Analytics cookies: Vercel Analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Advertising pixels: Meta Pixel for ad campaign optimization

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to the Service.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Objection: object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@riskpilot.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted third parties listed in Section 4 who help us operate the Service
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Safety: to protect the rights, property, or safety of RiskPilot, our users, or others

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Nigeria, including the United States (Supabase, Vercel) and Ireland (Meta). We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Children's Privacy

RiskPilot is not directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at support@riskpilot.app.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:
